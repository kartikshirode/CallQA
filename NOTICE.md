# Attribution

CallQA uses a subset of the HarperValley Bank corpus as its real WER/DER anchor.

The corpus is distributed under CC-BY-4.0, which requires attribution. We keep
the audio at its original 8 kHz telephone quality and do not modify it beyond
mixing the agent and caller channels into one mono track.

Citation:

> Mike Wu, Jonathan Nafziger, Anthony Scodary, Andrew Maas (2020).
> "HarperValleyBank: A Domain-Specific Spoken Dialog Corpus."
> arXiv:2010.13929.

Dataset repository:
https://github.com/cricketclub/gridspace-stanford-harper-valley
